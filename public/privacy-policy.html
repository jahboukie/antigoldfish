1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data We DON'T Collect

  • Your source code - Never transmitted or stored on our servers
  • AI conversations - All stored locally on your machine
  • Project files - Remain completely private on your device
  • Personal memories - Stored in local SQLite database only

1.2 Data We May Receive

  • Support communications - If you email us, we receive the content you send

2. How We Use Information

We use your support emails solely to respond to you.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Data Storage

All your sensitive data is stored locally on your machine:

  • SQLite database - Encrypted and stored in your user directory
  • Conversation history - Never transmitted to external servers
  • Project memories - Remain on your device at all times

3.2 Our Server Data

We don't collect server data for this product. We don't operate a backend for AGM.

4. Third-Party Services

AGM does not integrate with payment processors or analytics providers.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request information about data we have about you
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability - Export your local data (it's already on your machine!)
  • Opt-out - Disable analytics or marketing communications
  • Correction - Update or correct any personal information

6. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your machine until you delete it
  • Support data: Retained for up to 2 years for quality purposes

7. International Data Transfers

Since your sensitive data never leaves your machine, there are no international transfers of your code or conversations. Any minimal account data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

AntiGoldfishMode is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through the product interface.
